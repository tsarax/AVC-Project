This web app was done as part of MSIA 423 Analytics Value Chain class.The idea behind this app is to provide a helpful service for people on their house hunting journey in King County, Washington to get a feel for the potential cost of their home and how they can save some money.

City: These are the top 10 cities in King County and will only predict price in comparison to houses in the same city.
Bedrooms, Bathrooms, and Floors: These are the number of rooms and floors you would like in the house.
Waterfront: This indicates if you would like a waterfront view property. Kent and Auburn have no waterfront properties.
Condition: This is a number from 1-5 evaluated by the city to indicate house condition, 1 being poor(flip house) and 5 being great.
Basement: This indicates if you are expecting the home to have a basement.
Year Built: This allows you to indicate how old the house should be and/or what style (decade styles).
Renovations: This indicates if you are expcting the house to be renovated.
Lot sqft: This indicates the square footage of the lot the house is on.
